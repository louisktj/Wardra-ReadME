---

## What You Can Do with Wardra

### 1. Guided Onboarding
- Simple step-by-step onboarding to start quickly  
- Take a full-body photo of yourself  
- Add a few first garments to your closet so you can try Wardra right away  

### 2. Build Your Digital Closet
- Add clothes by category (tops, bottoms, shoes, etc.)  
- Use your camera or import from your gallery  
- Search and filter by category, color, or season  
- The app automatically seeds your closet with 3 sample pieces so you can test features instantly  

### 3. Create AI Looks on Your Own Model
- Pick a top, bottom, and other pieces in a Mix & Match builder  
- Wardra generates a realistic try-on image of the outfit on your own photo  
- You can save your favorite looks and reuse them later  
- Optional background cleanup for cleaner, more aesthetic images  

> All outfit images are generated by an AI backend powered by **Google Gemini 3**.

### 4. Plan Your Outfits in Advance
- Plan what you will wear for the next 3 weeks  
- See a calendar-style view of your upcoming outfits  
- Avoid repeating the same look and save time every morning  

### 5. Manage Looks and Wishlist
- Save multiple looks for each occasion (school, work, party, travel, etc.)  
- Create a visual wishlist for pieces you want to buy later  
- Quickly browse ideas when you donâ€™t know what to wear  

### 6. Comfortable Everyday Experience
- Light and dark themes that stay saved on your device  
- Smooth UI with premium fonts and gradients  
- Your data (closet and images) stays on your device thanks to local storage
